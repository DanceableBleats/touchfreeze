Why TouchFreeze?

Annoyed when you are typing a document and accidentally the palm of your hand brushes the touchpad, changing the position of the cursor in your document or accidently clicking on an option. TouchFreeze is simple utility for Windows NT/2000/XP that solves this problem. It automaticly disables touchpad while you are typing text. Like syndaemon on x-windows.
